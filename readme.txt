Integrate your WordPress forms with Zapier for automated lead capture and follow-up tasks, tailored for MaidCentral users.

== Description ==

Zapier Form Integration allows you to automate lead capture and follow-up tasks through Zapier. It's tailored for MaidCentral users who want to streamline their operations, but may work for other programs like L27.

= Key Features =
* Customizable form for lead capture
* Seamless integration with Zapier
* Automated workflows for lead management
* Tailored for MaidCentral users

= Use Cases for Zapier Integration =
* Automated Email Notifications: Set up a Zap to send yourself or your team an email whenever a form is submitted.
* Customer Engagement: Send an automated email or SMS to the customer after form submission.
* Lead Management: Automatically create a lead in MaidCentral to manage your sales pipeline more efficiently.
* Internal Communication: Use Zapier to send notifications or tasks to your employees when a form is submitted.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/zapier-form` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Use the Settings->Zapier Form screen to configure the plugin.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I set up the Zapier integration? =

1. Sign up or log in to Zapier.
2. Create a new Zap and choose "Webhooks by Zapier" as the trigger.
3. Set up the webhook and copy the provided URL.
4. Paste the webhook URL into the plugin's settings in WordPress.
5. Configure your desired actions in Zapier (e.g., creating leads in MaidCentral).

= What can I do with this integration? =

You can automate various tasks such as:
* Sending email notifications
* Creating leads in MaidCentral
* Sending SMS notifications
* Updating your CRM
* And much more, depending on your Zapier workflow
